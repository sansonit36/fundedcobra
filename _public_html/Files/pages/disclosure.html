Important information about the risks of trading

Effective Date: November 8, 2025

⚠️ Risk Warning

Trading financial instruments involves significant risk and may not be suitable for all investors. Past performance is not indicative of future results. You may lose some or all of your invested capital.

This Risk Disclosure Statement is provided by FundedCobra ("we," "our," or "us") to help you understand the risks associated with trading financial instruments through our platform. Please read this disclosure carefully before engaging in any trading activities.

Nature of Trading Risks

Trading financial instruments such as forex, commodities, indices, and cryptocurrencies carries a high level of risk and may not be suitable for all investors. The high degree of leverage that is often obtainable in trading can work against you as well as for you. The use of leverage can lead to large losses as well as gains.

Market Volatility

Financial markets are highly volatile and unpredictable. Prices can change rapidly due to various factors including economic events, political developments, market sentiment, and other external factors beyond our control. This volatility can result in significant losses.

Leverage Risk

Trading with leverage amplifies both potential profits and losses. While leverage can increase your returns, it can also result in losses that exceed your initial investment. You should only trade with capital you can afford to lose.

Specific Risks

  • Loss of Capital: You may lose some or all of the funds in your trading account.
  • Leverage Risk: Leverage can work against you, leading to substantial losses.
  • Market Risk: Prices can fluctuate rapidly and unpredictably.
  • Liquidity Risk: You may not be able to close positions at desired prices.
  • System Risk: Technical failures may affect your ability to trade.
  • Regulatory Risk: Changes in laws may impact trading conditions.
  • Psychological Risk: Trading decisions can be influenced by emotions.

Platform and Technology Risks

While we strive to maintain reliable systems, technical issues such as server failures, software bugs, internet connectivity problems, or other technological difficulties may occur. These issues could result in delayed executions, failed orders, or other trading disruptions that may cause losses.

Regulatory and Legal Risks

Trading regulations vary by jurisdiction and may change over time. Changes in regulatory requirements or legal interpretations could affect the availability of our services, the terms of trading, or the tax treatment of profits.

Disclaimer

FundedCobra does not provide investment advice. The information provided on our platform is for general informational purposes only and should not be construed as investment advice. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

We do not guarantee profits or freedom from losses. Trading involves substantial risk of loss and is not suitable for every investor.

Client Responsibilities

As a client, you are responsible for:

  • Understanding the risks involved in trading
  • Only investing capital you can afford to lose
  • Staying informed about market conditions
  • Using appropriate risk management strategies
  • Complying with all applicable laws and regulations

Acceptance of Terms

By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accepted the risks described in this disclosure. You agree that FundedCobra shall not be liable for any losses incurred as a result of trading activities.

Seek Independent Advice

We strongly recommend that you seek independent financial advice before engaging in any trading activities. If you do not fully understand the risks involved, you should not proceed with trading.
