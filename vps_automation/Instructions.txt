--- FundedCobra VPS Automation: Official Guide ---

Welcome to your professional Prop Firm Trading Factory. 
Everything is now organized in C:\FundedCobra.

1. INITIAL SETUP (One-Time)
   - Open PowerShell and run: C:\FundedCobra\vps_automation\setup\01_master_setup.ps1
   - This will build all 25 slots for you.

2. MASTER CONFIGURATION
   - Edit: C:\FundedCobra\vps_automation\config_master.json
   - Add your Master SMTP App Password and your website URLs here.

3. ADDING STOCK ACCOUNTS
   - Put your available Exness accounts in: C:\FundedCobra\Inbox\accounts_stock.txt
   - Format: Login,Password,Server (one per line).

4. STARTING THE AUTOMATION
   - Run: C:\FundedCobra\vps_automation\FundedCobra_Agent.py
   - Keep this window open! It is "The Brain" that watches for new sales.

5. HOW A SALE WORKS
   - When a sale happens (simulated by adding a buyer's email to C:\FundedCobra\Inbox\orders.txt), the Agent will:
     a) Pick the email.
     b) Pick an account from stock.
     c) Activate Slot_01.
     d) Configure SMTP and URLs.
     e) Launch MT5 instantly.

6. EMERGENCY TOOLS
   - Use: C:\FundedCobra\vps_automation\Emergency_Tools.ps1
   - Use this to check RAM, kill all MT5s, or update your Bot version across all slots.
