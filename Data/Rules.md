## Withdrawal Target & Payout Rules

### Special Instant Accounts ($1,250, $3,500, $5,000)
- **Withdrawal Target:** 5% of account size
- **No Profit Target Required** - Previous profit target system removed
- **Daily Payout Requests:** Can request payout anytime after hitting withdrawal target
- **Minimum Withdrawal:** $20 minimum to submit payout request
- **Profit Split:** 50% of profits available for withdrawal
- **Payout Processing:** Follows normal payout schedule (weekly on Saturdays)
- **Single Trade Limit:** A single trade cannot exceed 25% of the withdrawal target. If it does, payout for that cycle is reduced to 25%
  - Example: On a $1,250 account (5% withdrawal target = $62.50), if a single trade makes $16 or more (25% of $62.50), the payout will be capped at $15.62

### Standard Accounts (All Other Sizes)
- **Withdrawal Target:** 5% of account size (formerly 10% profit target)
- **Weekly Payouts:** Processed every Saturday
- **Minimum Trading Days:** Must trade at least 4 days per week
- **Minimum Withdrawal:** $20 minimum to submit payout request
- **Profit Split:** 50% of profits available for withdrawal
- **Single Trade Limit:** A single trade cannot exceed 25% of the withdrawal target. If it does, payout for that cycle is reduced to 25%
  - Example: On a $10,000 account (5% withdrawal target = $500), if a single trade makes $125 or more, the weekly payout will be capped at $125

## Drawdown Limits (All Accounts)
### Daily Drawdown
- Type: Trailing daily drawdown based on equity
- Limit: 8% from the highest equity point of the day
- Reset: Daily at market close
- Example:
  - Starting equity: $10,000
  - Peak equity during day: $10,500
  - Maximum allowed drawdown: $840 (8% of $10,500)
  - New peak equity: $11,000
  - New maximum allowed drawdown: $880 (8% of $11,000)

### Overall Drawdown
- Maximum allowed: 12% from initial equity
- Account termination if breached

## Prohibited Trading Practices
1. High-Frequency Trading (HFT)
2. Expert Advisors (EAs) or automated trading
3. Trades under 60 seconds duration
4. Martingale strategy
5. Grid trading
6. Arbitrage trading
7. One-sided trading (maximum 3 consecutive trades in same direction)
8. Hedging on same pair
9. Toxic trading patterns
10. Scalping under 1 minute

## Risk Management Requirements
- Stop loss recommended but not mandatory
- Maximum lot size based on account equity
- Position sizing must be reasonable relative to account size
- Risk per trade should not exceed 5% of account equity
